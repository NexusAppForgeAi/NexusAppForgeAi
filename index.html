Getting Started with AppForge

Welcome to AppForge - the AI-powered app builder that transforms your ideas into functional apps.

Quick Start

  1. Describe your app idea in the AI Generator
  2. Click "Generate App with AI"
  3. Preview your app in real-time
  4. Edit the code or regenerate
  5. Export as HTML, PWA, or APK

AI Providers

AppForge supports multiple AI providers:

  • Puter.js (Default) - Free, no API key needed
  • OpenAI - GPT-4 for advanced generation
  • Claude - Anthropic's AI assistant
  • Gemini - Google's AI model
  • DeepSeek - Open source alternative
