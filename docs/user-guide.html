Getting Started

Welcome to AppForge - the most powerful AI-driven app builder. This guide will help you create stunning web apps, PWAs, and mobile apps in minutes.

Describe Your App

Start by describing what you want to build in the AI Generator. Be specific about features, design, and functionality. The more details you provide, the better the result!

Generate with AI

Click "Generate App with AI" and watch as our Deep-Seek Technology transforms your description into a complete, functional app.

Preview & Edit

See your app in the live preview. Edit the code directly in our powerful editor with syntax highlighting and AI debugging.

Export & Share

Export as HTML, PWA, or APK. Share with the community or deploy to production!

AI Providers

AppForge supports multiple AI providers. Choose the one that works best for you:

  • Puter.js (Grok) - Free, no API key needed
  • OpenAI (GPT-4) - Most powerful, requires your API key
  • Claude (Anthropic) - Excellent for complex apps
  • Google Gemini - Fast and reliable
  • DeepSeek - Open source alternative

FAQ

Is AppForge really free?

Yes! The free tier includes unlimited AI generations and HTML exports. Upgrade to Pro for APK builds and more features.

Who owns the apps I create?

You do! All apps you create are 100% yours. We don't claim any ownership.

Can I sell apps I make with AppForge?

Absolutely! Apps created with AppForge are yours to sell, distribute, or monetize however you like.

How do I get featured in the gallery?

Submit your app through the "Submit Your App" button. Our team reviews submissions and features the best ones!
